Project Description

SportMeet is an application designed to address a common problem faced by sports enthusiasts: the difficulty in finding partners or groups to participate in their preferred sports activities. This issue is particularly common in individual or small-team sports like tennis, basketball, and football, where players often struggle with finding partners and booking suitable venues.

The app simplifies the process of coordinating sporting activities by allowing users to discover partners based on similar sports interests, book public or private fields, and manage logistics such as splitting costs and ensuring attendance. The application also provides users with the ability to communicate via in-app chat, helping them organize their activities efficiently.

Target Users

SportMeet is designed for amateur athletes and sports enthusiasts of all levels. The app is especially useful for casual players, fitness-focused individuals, university students, and young professionals who may not have established sports groups or partners. Additionally, it aims to help people new to a location find others with shared sports interests.

Project Goals

  • Enable users to select their preferred sport and find public or private fields in their area.
  • Provide real-time availability and pricing for private sports venues.
  • Help users find sports partners or groups by posting requests for teammates or partners.
  • Facilitate in-app communication to coordinate logistics, split costs, and confirm attendance.
  • Integrate social features, allowing users to connect based on shared sports interests.

SportMeet aims to merge sports scheduling functionalities with social networking aspects, enhancing both convenience and the sense of community for sports lovers.
