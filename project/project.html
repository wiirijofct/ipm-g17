Project Overview

SportMeet is an application designed to address the common problem of finding sports partners or groups to participate in various activities. This is especially relevant for individual or small-team sports such as tennis, basketball, and football. Often, coordinating schedules and booking facilities can be a hassle.

The app allows users to:

  • Select their preferred sport and search for public or private fields in their area.
  • View available time slots and pricing for private fields.
  • Post requests for partners or teammates to form a group.
  • Use an in-app chat to coordinate logistics, split costs, and ensure attendance.

SportMeet enhances the experience by combining scheduling functionality with social networking elements, allowing users to connect based on their shared sports interests.
